Thank You for choosing CT Food as your supplier.

CT Food is a market leading import and distribution company which specializes in authentic foodstuff of well-known brands from all exotic corners of South-East Asia. With over 25 years of experience and expertise, an extensive network of suppliers, clients and a sophisticated logistics system, CT Food has steadily grown into a credible distributor in the Nordic countries.

It is Your confidence and support in CT Food that continuously advances and encourages us to take on new exciting challenges and expand the company and product assortment to cater to our customers’ needs and expectations.

CT Food’s flexibility allows us to put the customer as our main focus and offer them a customized service. Our dedicated staff is multilingual and proficient in Swedish, English, Cantonese, Mandarin, Vietnamese, Teo Chew and Thai, and are able to tailor-make offers and services especially for your requirements and needs.

The ordering process is streamlined and conveniently placed over the telephone, fax or e-mail.We ensure that our customers feel happy and comfortable with their choice of having CT Food as their supplier!
